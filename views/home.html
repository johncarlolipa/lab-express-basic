Welcome to Ricky Rubio's Home Page

Professional Basketball Player

Ricky Rubio is a Spanish professional basketball player currently playing for the Minnesota Timberwolves in the NBA. Born on October 21, 1990, in El Masnou, Spain, Ricky has made significant contributions to the sport and is widely recognized for his impressive skills as a point guard.

Throughout his career, Ricky has showcased exceptional passing abilities, court vision, and leadership on the basketball court. He has played for various teams in the NBA, including the Utah Jazz and Phoenix Suns.
